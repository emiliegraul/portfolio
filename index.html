Colour Studio

Project Manager & Design Associate

2017 - 2019

Deliverables included thorough diagrams of building details like these unique facades seen around San Francisco.

The Colour Studio is a boutique color consultancy that has operated in San Francisco for over forty years. The company works primarily with architecture firms and property management companies in California, supporting the massive development in the area. As Project Manager and Design Associate, I was responsible for leading all ongoing projects from start to finish.

My responsibilities at the company included generating proposals, outlining scope, delegating project tasks, and maintaining daily coordination with clients, designers, architects, developers, contractors, et al. I also had a hand in creating and developing design choices and presenting them to clients.

One of the highlights of my experience there was working with the older, more ornately-styled buildings of San Francisco. We enjoyed designing bright and intricate schemes for these unique properties. It’s now a pleasure of mine to spot building exteriors around the city that I had a hand in creating.

At every step, colors were meticulously matched from digital screens, to ink prints, to final paint applications, guaranteeing an accurate portrayal from start to finish.

During my time at Colour Studio I drove many projects to completion, including multiple 100+ unit residential projects around San Francisco, Oakland, Mountain View, San Diego, and Los Angeles.

Our projects ranged in size from single unit Victorians in residential neighborhoods, to multi-million dollar renovations like that of the UCSF campus.

Hack Reactor

Operations Associate

2015 - 2017

Hack Reactor is a premier coding bootcamp in San Francisco. During a three month program, students are trained as software engineers and guided into the industry. As an Operations Associate, I had a front-facing role with students and coworkers.

Students paired up in our office working on coding challenges. Our operations team supported 4 cohorts of 40+ students at a time.

Constant office upkeep was critical as students and staff would be in the space from early morning to late at night. It was important to ensure everything was properly prepared for the 50+ staff members and 200+ students.

As the company grew and acquired smaller bootcamp start-ups, I assisted in the moving and opening of 3 locations.

By the way! Before the pandemic I spent 5 months traveling around the world, visiting 12 different countries. I met some amazing people, stayed in some beautiful cities, and ate some delicious food. I learned a lot about different cultures, but also about myself in the process. Ask me about it!
